Chicken Schnitzel with Roasted Vegetables

Description

A quick and healthy chicken schnitzel dish served with oven-roasted vegetables. High in protein and low in carbs, this recipe is perfect for busy days. Feel free to add your favorite spices and a splash of lemon juice for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken schnitzels (store-bought or homemade)
  • 300–400g (10–14 oz) mixed pre-cut oven-ready vegetables (e.g. bell peppers, zucchini, carrots)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (plus extra for frying)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (or mixed herbs)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon juice (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Place the vegetables in an oven-safe dish. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, sprinkle with Italian seasoning, and toss to coat evenly.
  3. Roast the vegetables for 25–30 minutes, stirring halfway through to ensure even cooking.
  4. When the vegetables have about 10 minutes left, heat a bit of olive oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat.
  5. Cook the schnitzels for about 8 minutes, flipping every 2 minutes, until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Serve the schnitzels with the roasted vegetables. Optionally, drizzle with fresh lemon juice before serving.
