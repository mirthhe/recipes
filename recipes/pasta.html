Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A quick and hearty spaghetti bolognese made with minced beef and pre-made tomato sauce. Perfect for busy weeknights or meal prepping. Simple ingredients, big flavor.

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) dried spaghetti
  • 250g (9 oz) minced beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 jar (about 400g / 14 oz) of your favorite tomato-based pasta sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the minced beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  5. Once browned, pour in the tomato sauce. Stir well and let it simmer for 5â€“10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce and toss to combine evenly.
  7. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan if desired.
