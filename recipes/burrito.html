Loaded Personal Burrito

Description

This is a hearty, flavor-packed burrito filled with seasoned beef, fresh veggies, and crunchy extras. It’s easy to customize, quick to prepare, and makes for an incredibly satisfying meal—especially with pesto and nacho crunch for a personal twist.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Bell peppers, chopped
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Black beans, rinsed and drained
  • Corn (canned or fresh), drained
  • Black olives, sliced
  • Tortilla wraps
  • Burrito seasoning mix or taco spices
  • Pesto (your favorite kind)
  • Grated cheese (e.g. cheddar or a mix)
  • Fresh lettuce, shredded
  • Nachos, lightly crushed

Steps

  1. In a large pan, cook the minced beef over medium heat until browned. Add burrito seasoning and a splash of water, then stir to coat.
  2. Add chopped bell peppers and halved cherry tomatoes to the pan. Cook for 5–7 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in black beans and corn. Cook for another 2–3 minutes to heat through.
  4. Mix in the sliced olives and remove the pan from heat.
  5. Warm the tortilla wraps in a dry pan or microwave for 10–20 seconds until pliable.
  6. To assemble: spread pesto on the wrap, add a scoop of the beef-veggie mixture, then top with shredded lettuce, grated cheese, and crushed nachos.
  7. Wrap tightly and enjoy immediately, or toast in a pan for extra crispiness.
